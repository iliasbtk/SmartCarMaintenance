About

             Menu        

This app helps you manage your car’s maintenance and track the costs. You are able to track your car maintenance schedules such as oil and filter changes, tire rotations, brake checks and brake fluid changes, interim and full services. In addition, the App notifies you about important expiring insurance, MOT, and road tax documents.

First you enter your vehicle registration number, vehicle model, make and current mileage in addition to the expiry date of the car’s insurance, MOT, and road tax.

After that you can update the required mileage or days for the next maintenance schedule for predefined maintenance such as oil and filter changes, tire rotations, brake checks and brake fluid changes. Then each time you do maintenance you will have to enter it alongside with the updated mileage.

Based on the information above, the app creates a maintenance schedule and sets up email reminders for upcoming services and expiring documents, the user needs to manually input each task performed such as services, document renewals or any other expenses. Mileage needs to be updated regularly.

Vehicle services such as fuel cost, parking, car wash and fines can be entered manually to be tracked with the vehicle cost which are summarised by vehicle, by service type or by maintenance type.

This app can also be used by professionals such as taxi drivers or a tradesperson who uses a vehicle in their job (e.g. van) as the app allows them to track the cost and maintenance.

Finally, the management of multiple vehicles by a single user of the app is also supported.
